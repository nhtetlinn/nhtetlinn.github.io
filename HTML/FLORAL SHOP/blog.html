About Us

Working as a florist in Myanmar is an exciting and sometimes unpredicatable place. We work hard and have fun, meet
interseting people and most importantly get to be creative through interesting briefs, beautiful venues, fantastic, clients
and of course wonderful, colourful flowers.

Naing Floral is born out of this rish experience. The concept is simple. i will provide strong designs with high quality
flowers, delivered and managed with exceptional service.

Allow me to bring this world to life for you.
